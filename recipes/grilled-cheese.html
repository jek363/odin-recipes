Grilled Cheese

Simple lunch dish of grilled bread and cheese

A simple lunch staple. Cheese in between two slices of bread and grilled in a pan. Serves 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese of choice
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise

Directions:

  1. Heat pan to medium high heat
  2. Spread mayonnaise on 1 side of each slice of bread
  3. Place both slices of cheese in between bread, with the mayonnaise on the outside.
  4. Add sandwich to pan and flip when toasted
