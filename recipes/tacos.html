Tacos

Mexican dish consisting of ground beef and various toppings on a tortilla

Ground beef cooked with taco seasoning on a corn tortilla. Topped with cilantro, chopped onions, and lime juice. Serves 6

Taco Meat Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 0.5 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 0.75 cup of water

Directions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook, breaking up clumps with a spatula, until fat renders out and beef begins to brown, about 4 minutes. Add onion and cook, stirring constantly until onion is softened and beef is crumbly, about 4 minutes. Drain fat if desired.
  2. Add desired amount of taco seasoning and cook 1 minute. Add in tomato paste and water and cook, using a spoon to loosen any browned bits from the pan, and stir until well combined. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.
